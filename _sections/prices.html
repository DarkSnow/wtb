We always offer a very competitive price, based on your needs for the day and any options or special requests you have. We go out of our way to be as flexible as possible. Our Pricing is based on a number of factors that include location, time of year and weekend or mid-week event.

Please contact us by phone or email (or meet us at a show/exhibition) to discuss what video services you need for your Wedding Day.

If you‘re not 100% sure what you want regarding the Wedding Video, then we offer a free hand-holding service to discuss your options with the aim of finding a happy match between your video budget and a Wedding Video that meets your wishes.

The price includes

  • Pre-wedding discussions and/or meeting
  • One cameraman with 2 cameras, one manned and one unmanned
  • Professional audio equipment for the recording of the ceremony/speeches etc.
  • Editing and authoring of the DVD
  • Full menu/selectable chapter structure on the DVD
  • 3 copies of the Wedding DVD, in personalised cases

      • Typical DVD Chapters

      • Intro featuring the wedding venue and reception locations (to music)
      • The guests arriving for the wedding ceremony (to music)
      • The bride-to-be arriving (to music)
      • The wedding ceremony
      • The photo session (to music)
      • All the speeches
      • Video messages from the wedding guests, if time allows
      • The first dances, up to 9 pm (you guessed it… to music!)
      • The very popular wedding day highlights (to music)

      We‘ll ask you to choose a selection of music tracks for the relevant parts of the DVD. We‘re happy to advise you on which tracks work and which don‘t. If you want us to choose them all, then that‘s fine too. Any subsequent changes after delivery are chargeable.

      The duration of your DVD is usually between one and two hours long, being dependant to a great extent on the lengths of the ceremony and the speeches, and any customisations or extras you may chose.
